Key insights
⚠️
33% gave 1/10
The single largest rating group is the lowest possible score. This points to a significant number of extremely poor experiences rather than mild dissatisfaction.
🍽️
Food is the biggest pain point
Food & beverages scored 2.66/5 — the lowest of all service categories — suggesting this is where BA is losing passenger trust most consistently.
💺
Business class underperforms economy
Business class average rating (3.93) is lower than Economy (4.36) and First Class (4.39), indicating premium passengers have higher expectations that BA is failing to meet.
